How to Register

Registration Methods

The TRAIN 2004 Conference is now over, so there's no point in submitting any registration forms to us.

Online Online registration is the recommended method.
  • All online registrants must have an e-mail address.
  • A group of people can be registered, then all paid for at once.
  • Payment is by Visa or MasterCard only.
See our privacy policy.
Mail-In Those who don't have access to the Internet or don't have e-mail addresses can register by mail.
  • Obviously, it's a slightly slower method than online registration.
  • All registration forms for a group must be sent in a single envelope.
  • Payment is by cheque only.
Get the registration form here (542 KB).
Walk-In If the online registration is closed and it's too late to mail in a registration form then you can still show up at the conference and register in person.
  • Seminars do fill up, so there's a risk that you won't be able to register for the seminars you want. Have second or even third choices ready just in case.
  • Payment is by cash, cheque, Visa or MasterCard.
Get the registration form here (542 KB).

Keynote Speaker & Worship Leader

Bruce Merrifield

Bruce Merrifield was born and raised in Northern Alberta. After graduating from the University of Alberta with a degree in history/philosophy, and from NABC (now Taylor Seminary) with a degree in theology, Bruce served as a solo pastor, a church planter, and a senior pastor. Currently based in Costa Mesa, CA, Bruce works with the organization Vantage Point3, training and equipping pastors across North America to develop emerging leaders in their churches. It is his desire to see these growing leaders "triumph in the trenches!"

Doug Horley

A singer and songwriter of such songs as "We Want to See Jesus Lifted High" and "King of Heaven," Doug brings his own brand of exuberance to conferences. He travels widely, leading family Praise Parties, seminars and adult celebrations. Look forward to an engaging praise and worship experience!
