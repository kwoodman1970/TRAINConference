J-Train – "I Got A Ticket!"

If you are a youth minister or youth sponsor, plan to attend this exciting seminar track with your teen youth group leaders. Robin Johnson, Scott Bakken, Matt Wilks and Dom Ruso will equip your teen leaders in outreach, spiritual growth and ministry training.

Student rates are available. Youth ministers and sponsors may attend free of charge. Please fill out a registration form, clearly indicating that you are a youth sponsor or minister.

Session A-15
Friday, 8:15pm

1st Stop: Search Engine

...type in a few words and see what it finds. Leadership is about the heart. Experience worship, confession, and communion.

Session B-15
Saturday, 9:00am

2nd Stop: Join the Ride

...who is walking with you beside the J-Train? Leadership is about influence.

Session C-15
Saturday, 11:15am

3rd Stop: Up Grade

...from coach to 1st class is a huge difference; it's time to upgrade your spiritual walk with Jesus. Leadership is about growing and developing yourself.

Session D-15
Saturday, 2:00pm

4th Stop: Return Ticket

...make room for souvenirs; there are tons to take home. Leadership is about listening and implementing.
