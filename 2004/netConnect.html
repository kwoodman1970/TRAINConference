About NetConnect

NetConnect is an exciting new feature of TRAIN Conference. Here you'll be able to connect and network with others in your area of ministry.

Start by joining us during Saturday's lunch break for an informal networking time. Meet with fellow believers who share a passion for your area of ministry and:

  • share your experiences and successes
  • learn from their experiences and successes
  • discuss common issues and concerns
  • get to know other members of the Body of Christ

Be sure to check for details on discussion topics in the TRAIN 2004 Conference Booklet when you arrive.

But it doesn't end there! Stay in touch with your networking contacts in the NetConnect Forums. Post messages for all to see – questions, answers, announcements, warnings, or whatever you need to discuss. You can carry on where you left off at the conference, or raise a completely new issue.

Go beyond the conference – way beyond the conference – and tap into one of God's best resources: the Body of Christ!
