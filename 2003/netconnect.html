NetConnect

About NetConnect

NetConnect is an exciting new feature of TRAIN Conference. Here you'll be able to connect and network with others in your area of ministry.

NetConnect uses the bulletin-board model for communication – that is, a person posts a message for all to see, and others can post further messages to respond to it. Do you have a question? Post a message! Do you have an answer? Post a message! Do you have an announcement? Post a message! Do you have a warning? Post a message!

So go beyond the conference – way beyond the conference – and tap into one of God's best resources: the Body of Christ!

Join us during Saturday's lunch break for an informal networking time. Meet with fellow believers who share a passion for your area of ministry and:

  • Share your experience and successes
  • Learn from their experiences and successes
  • Discuss common issues and concerns
  • Get to know other members of the Body of Christ

Be sure to check for details on discussion topics in the TRAIN 2003 Conference Booklet when you arrive.
