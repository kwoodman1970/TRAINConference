Stand Firm! Speak Boldly! Serve Faithfully!

We are living at a time in history when Biblical truth is under attack – or at the very least, Biblical truth is being marginalized and is largely seen as irrelevant in our world.

As Christians, and as the Church of the Living God, we should rightly ask ourselves how we ought to respond to "such a time as this." If we read God's Word, we will quickly realize that this is not a time to shrink back from declaring the Truth.

In Ephesians, the apostle Paul continually reminds us of who we are in Christ. He also reminds us that we are in a battle for Truth. Taking these two facts together, we are exhorted to stand firm (6:13-14), to speak boldly (6:19-20) and to serve faithfully (6:21). TRAIN 2005 invites you to join together as the greater Church to learn how we can more effectively stand, speak and serve.

We are excited to be able to offer some brand new seminar tracks to equip you to serve in your church. In addition, we are pleased to be able to partner this year with The Leadership Centre Willow Creek Canada to offer you a bonus day on Friday at the Creating A Volunteer Revolution conference at no additional cost to you.

Be sure to take advantage of all we have to offer to make TRAIN 2005 a rich time of being equipped to serve the Kingdom of God. See you in November!

Dan Sudfeld
Chair, TRAIN 2005
