Guest Speaker

Lou Leventhal

Internationally acclaimed Illusionist Lou Leventhal will present his fun and highly entertaining program, "Illusions & Reality", on Friday, November 3 at 7:30pm. Unbelievable stage illusions, live animals, audience participation, original music, infectious humour and inspiring spiritual truths are orchestrated into an exciting and fast-paced show that you won't want to miss. This extremely popular program has captivated audiences on stages including Sea World, Six Flags over Texas, Magic Mountain in California, World EXPO in Vancouver, and in auditoriums all over the world.

Leventhal demonstrates how easily even the most intelligent audiences can be fooled by natural means. From the outset of the program Leventhal makes it clear that he doesn't possess any special powers, nor is he involved with the occult in any way. He shares that his personal relationship with God through Jesus Christ is the foundational reality in his life.

Come, laugh, and be renewed in your spirit with Lou's amazing presentation. Bring your family and friends with you for this special event!

About Lou Leventhal: Lou was raised in an Orthodox Jewish home and came to Christ at the age of 18. He has a dynamic walk with the Lord and a vibrant faith. He communicates God's Word with clarity, power, practical application and humour to both non-believers and Christians alike.

Tickets: This event is included with your early-bird registration to TRAIN 2006. Tickets can also be purchased separately for this event only, at $10 per ticket, on a first-come, first-served basis. Please note: if you register after the early bird registration deadline (October 16), you are not guaranteed a seat for this opening session, so be sure to register early this year!
