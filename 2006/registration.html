Registration Information

The TRAIN 2006 Conference is now over, so there's no point in trying to register for it.

How To Register And/Or Purchase Tickets

Registration for the conference includes admission to Lou Leventhal's "Illusions & Reality". Those who register by the early-bird deadline of October 16 are guarenteed admission; after that, admission is on a first-come, first-served basis.

If you purchase tickets just for Lou Leventhal's "Illusions & Reality" before October 25 then they will be mailed to you; after that, they can be picked up on Friday at the door. We reserve the right to limit the number of tickets that can be purchased by an individual.

There are three ways to register and/or purchase tickets:

Online Online registration is the recommended method.
  • All online registrants must have an e-mail address.
  • A group of people can be registered, then all paid for at once.
  • Payment is by Visa or MasterCard only.
See our privacy policy.
Mail-In Those who don't have access to the Internet or don't have e-mail addresses can register by mail.
  • Obviously, it's a slightly slower method than online registration.
  • All registration forms for a group must be sent in a single envelope.
  • Payment is by cheque only.

Get the registration form here (490 KB).

Walk-In If the online registration is closed and it's too late to mail in a registration form then you can still show up at the conference and register in person.
  • Seminars do fill up, so there's a risk that you won't be able to register for the seminars you want. Have second or even third choices ready just in case.
  • Payment is by cash, cheque, Visa or MasterCard (those paying by credit card must have an e-mail address).

Get the registration form here (490 KB).
